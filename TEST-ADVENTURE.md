# Simple D&D Test Adventure

**For**: Testing the D&D MCP Server
**Duration**: 15-30 minutes
**Level**: 1st level character

## Quick Character

**Name**: Theron  
**Race**: Human  
**Class**: Fighter (Level 1)  
**HP**: 12  
**AC**: 16 (chain mail + shield)  
**Weapon**: Longsword (1d8+3)

**Ability Scores**:
- STR 16 (+3)
- DEX 12 (+1)  
- CON 14 (+2)
- INT 10 (+0)
- WIS 13 (+1)
- CHA 8 (-1)

**Skills**: Athletics +5, Perception +3

## The Scenario

You're a guard for a small merchant caravan traveling through the Greenwood Forest. It's late afternoon, and you're walking alongside the lead wagon when you hear rustling in the bushes ahead.

## Encounter: Goblin Ambush!

Two goblins leap out from the undergrowth, wielding crude daggers!

**Goblin Stats** (each):
- AC: 15 (leather armor + shield)
- HP: 7 each
- Attack: Scimitar +4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage
- DEX save: +2

## How to Play

Ask me to:
1. **Roll initiative** - "Roll 1d20+1 for my initiative"
2. **Make attacks** - "I attack the goblin with my longsword, roll 1d20+5"
3. **Check rules** - "What's the rule for opportunity attacks?"
4. **Resolve situations** - "Can I shove the goblin as a bonus action?"

## Using MCP Tools

During the game, I'll use:
- `roll_dice` - For all rolls
- `query_rule` - To check rules
- `resolve_edge_case` - For unusual situations

## Ready to Start?

Just say: **"Let's begin! Roll initiative for me."**

Or if you want to create your own character, just describe them and we'll start!
